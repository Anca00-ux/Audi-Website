Technology and Performance

Motor Puternic

Audi engines are known for their blend of power, efficiency, and innovative technology. They come in various configurations, including four-cylinder, V6, and V8 options, with turbocharging and supercharging for improved performance. Audi engines often feature direct fuel injection, lightweight construction, and Quattro all-wheel-drive systems in many models, delivering a dynamic driving experience with a reputation for both performance and fuel efficiency.

Quattro technology

Quattro technology is a brand name used by the German automobile manufacturer Audi to refer to its all-wheel-drive (AWD) system. Audi introduced the Quattro system in the early 1980s and has since become renowned for its performance and traction capabilities, particularly in challenging road conditions.In summary, Quattro technology is Audi's renowned all-wheel-drive system, known for its ability to provide superior traction, stability, and performance in various driving conditions.

Virtual Cockpit

Audi’s Virtual Cockpit may sound like something out of a space simulator, but it’s such an amazing addition to your car that you’ll wonder how you ever managed without it. The Audi Virtual Cockpit replaces your standard analogue driver’s instruments with a set of digital dials on a 12.3-inch display tucked neatly behind the steering wheel. It’ll display all sorts of things like your speedometer, rev counter, 3D navigation software, radio, media information, assorted driver assistance systems, caller ID and car settings.
